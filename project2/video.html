The Vital Role of Water in the Human Body

Water is essential for our bodies, making up over 60% of our composition and playing crucial roles such as cushioning joints, regulating temperature, and nourishing vital organs. To stay healthy, adequate hydration is necessary, reflecting water's importance from birth to adulthood.
